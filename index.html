Privacy Policy for NeutralView

Last updated: October 22, 2024

Introduction

This privacy policy explains how NeutralView ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Chrome extension.

Information Collection

Our extension collects the following information when you analyze an article:

  • Article URL
  • Article content for analysis purposes
  • Analysis results

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for:

  • Analyzing article bias
  • Improving our analysis algorithm
  • Providing you with analysis results

Data Storage

All analysis data is stored locally on your device. We do not maintain any central database of analyzed articles or user data.

Third-Party Services

We use AI services to analyze article content. The text is processed securely and is not stored by these services after analysis.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes through the extension.
